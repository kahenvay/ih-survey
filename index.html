Enterprise Data Collection

Who is this for?

This section is for founders, directors, CEOs, and enterprise leaders.

Why share your data?

By providing insights about your enterprise, you help us track impact, environment, and diversity metrics while unlocking new opportunities for your business.

📌 Hosted on Good Market, a not-for-profit digital commons that allows you to control your data and avoid re-entering information. If you already have a profile, some responses will be pre-filled.

Benefits of joining Good Market:

  • Increase visibility for your enterprise
  • Gain Good Market approval & opt into People & Planet First Verification
  • Share products, services, job vacancies, fundraising campaigns, and more
  • Connect with potential partners, customers, and investors
  • Access curated marketplaces & procurement opportunities

🔒 Privacy: Responses are stored under Impact Hub GmbH's privacy policy and anonymised for reporting. Financial details remain confidential. By clicking Continue, you consent to sharing your data with Impact Hub GmbH.
